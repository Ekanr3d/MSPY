What is mSpy?

mSpy is a number one monitoring software for smartphones and computers that helps parents to keep children safe and workers productive. It keeps tabs on text messages, call information, GPS coordinates and much more depending on your needs and priorities. mSpy collects information from the target device and sends it to your Control Panel (your account that will be created after purchase) which you can access from any browser. mSpy for smartphones runs on Android 4+ rooted/not rooted and iOS 6 – 8.3 jailbroken/non-jailbroken based platforms.

User-friendly Customer’s area, 24/7 live Customer Support, vast variety of subscription plans and affordable prices would make your experience the best value for your money.

How does mSpy work?

mSpy collects data from the device on which it is installed and displays it in the Control Panel which you can access using any Internet browser.

To start using mSpy, you need to check the compatibility of the device you’d like to monitor, choose the subscription plan and proceed with the purchase. After that you receive an email with the login and password to your personal Control Panel where you also find the installation instructions. Basically you’ll need to have a full authorized physical access to the target device, launch the phone browser, type in the link for downloading the program and proceed with the installation. By installing mSpy, you confirm your intention to use this software legally, i.e. you install mSpy on your own device, the device of your underage child or the owner of the device agrees to be monitored.

Does mSpy work worldwide?

Our monitoring software works regardless of the cellular carrier network or the location of the target device and is compatible with all service providers (carriers). However, mSpy requires Internet connection to transfer data to your personal account (Control Panel - your account that will be created after purchase), thus mobile Internet or Wi-Fi connection must be switched on the target device.

How do I install mSpy?

mSpy Without Jailbreak

If your target phone is an iPhone and you do not want to jailbreak it, you can use mSpy Without Jailbreak that requires users's iCloud credentials for installation. You will just need to log in to your Control Panel and choose a proper option that leads you through few simple steps of installation. iPhone back up has to be enabled on the device. Please, be informed that iPhones back up to iCloud once a day only if the following conditions are met: - the device is connected to WiFi; - the device is plugged in; - the device is locked.

If iCloud Backup is not switched on the device, please activate it manually in Settings > iCloud > iCloud Backup > On. Then go to Backup section and hit Back Up Now for mSpy to make the initial synchronization of the data from the target device to your Control Panel.

Regular mSpy application for iOS

In order to install regular mSpy application and have permission to all available features, you need to jailbreak the target device.  First of all, you will have to check if your device can be jailbroken because some newer versions of iOS may not have jailbreak available yet. To check it, you can take target device and open Settings > General > About > Version and check all the needed instructions and latest jailbreaking tools (versions) here: Once jailbreak is done, you can simply log in to your Control Panel and choose iPhone to continue installation. Whole procedure of jailbreak and installation may take up to 30 minutes*

mSpy for Android based OS

To install mSpy to an Android device, you need to have an authorized physical access to the device and choose Android in your Control Panel to see the instructions. Please, be informed that mSpy can be installed without rooting. But if you wish to monitor Whatsapp, Viber, Facebook, Skype, Line, Snapchat and Gmail, your target Android phone should be rooted*

*If you are experiencing difficulties with an installation or having troubles with setting up the features we’ll gladly perform initial installation of mSpy on your target mobile device with our special service mAssistance! How often does mSpy information gets updated?

Using mSpy Without Jailbreak, you receive information within 24 hour since the latest back up has been done. Usually it takes that much time because backups can reach up to few gigabytes and it depends on WiFi connection quality.

If you have mSpy on a jailbroken iPhone or Android, you can expect to receive updates based on the Update Interval you have selected. The Update Interval specifies how often mSpy is connected to our server and transfers information directly to your Control Panel. When manually adjusting Update Interval from your account, please note that short update intervals (less than 10 min) may consume considerable battery life. Please, be informed that sometimes it can take up to 24 hours to receive all updates especially if the phone constantly uses 3G/4G connection which is not very stable and always depends on signal strength.

Jailbreaking procedure doesn’t affect phone’s performance and in many cases it even extends battery usage of the device.
